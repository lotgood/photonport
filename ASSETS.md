# Asset licensing and source policy

The PhotonPort-specific app icons under `Mac/Assets.xcassets` and
`iOS/Assets.xcassets` were added in commit `bef089c` and are copyright © 2026
hyupji. They are distributed under GPL-3.0-only with the rest of PhotonPort.

The repository currently contains the complete raster asset catalog used by the
build. The editable source artwork was not present in this checkout when the
public-release audit was performed. Future icon or branding changes must commit
the preferred editable source form (for example SVG, layered artwork, and the
generation/export instructions) together with generated PNGs.

Upstream OpenDisplay assets that remain in git history retain their original
copyright and license notices.
